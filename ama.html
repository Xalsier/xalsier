Favorite Soulsborne or Soulslike

I've played Dark Souls 3, Bloodbourne, Lies of P and Elden Ring. I don't think I've finished Demon Souls or Black Myth: Wukong yet.

Out of the four games that I have finished playing through, I think my favorite is Lies of P. The reason I say that is it's the only soulslike I've played through 4-5 times. If I were to give an answer on a favorite soulsborne, it'd be Bloodborne.

Admittedly, I do have dreams of writing or working on a world that is something like a soulslike... But I do need more experience before I can give a project like that proper justice.
