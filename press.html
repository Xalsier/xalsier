My Bio

25 Words

Xalsier (b. 2003), also known as Xalsier Kitsune, is an American visual artist, author, and volunteer Wikipedia editor from Florida.

50 Words

Xalsier (born February 1, 2003), also known as Xalsier Kitsune, is the debut author of "Swallow Pines: One-shot", an adult short story featuring anthro characters and speculative alt-history. He is a digital artist, who has been producing work for the furry fandom since 2022.

100 Words

Xalsier (b. 2003), full name Xalsier Kitsune, is an Hispanic American author, digital artist, and contributor to Wikipedia. His work began in 2022, in Florida, with character illustrations and sketches posted online, but he has since began experimenting with vector art as well as more fully rendered pieces for book covers and concept art.

On Wikipedia he has over 4000 edits on his belt. He is involved in the furry subculture with his works, with his more recent book "Swallow Pines: One-shot" being largely furry based speculative fiction.
